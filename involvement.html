Get Involved

Join our community of volunteers and make a difference in immigrant students' lives

Volunteer Opportunities

Academic Mentor

Work one-on-one with immigrant students to provide academic support and guidance.

2-4 hours per week
Flexible scheduling
Virtual or in-person
  • College degree or current student
  • Patience and empathy
  • Commitment to 6+ months

Communication Coach

Help students improve their English communication skills through conversation practice.

1-2 hours per week
Weekly sessions
Virtual preferred
  • Native English speaker
  • Teaching experience helpful
  • Cultural sensitivity

Event Coordinator

Help organize workshops, fundraisers, and community events for our organization.

5-10 hours per month
Event-based
Local community
  • Organizational skills
  • Event planning experience
  • Strong communication
