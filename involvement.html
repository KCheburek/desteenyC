Ways to Get Involved

Skill Teacher

Share your expertise by teaching others your skills - coding, cooking, languages, art, or anything you're passionate about.

1-3 hours per session
Flexible scheduling
Virtual or in-person
  • Proficiency in your skill
  • Enthusiasm for teaching
  • Patience and clear communication

Skill Learner

Join our community to learn new skills from others. Everyone is welcome, regardless of experience level.

1-2 hours per session
Regular sessions
Virtual or in-person
  • Open mind and willingness to learn
  • Basic commitment to attend sessions
  • Respect for teachers and other learners

Community Volunteer

Join our group volunteer activities with local organizations to make a positive impact in our community.

2-4 hours per event
Monthly events
Local community
  • Team spirit and collaboration
  • Reliability and punctuality
  • Desire to help others

How to Apply

1

4
