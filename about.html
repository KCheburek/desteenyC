About Desteeny

Learn about our story, mission, and the team behind our organization

Our Story

Desteeny was founded in 2018 by a group of educators and community leaders who recognized the unique challenges faced by immigrant families in the United States. We saw that many talented students were struggling not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked the support systems and guidance needed to navigate the complex American education system.

What started as a small mentorship program has grown into a comprehensive organization that serves hundreds of immigrant families each year. Our name "Desteeny" represents our commitment to helping students reach their full potential and achieve their dreams.

Our Programs

Communication Workshops

Weekly workshops focused on improving English communication skills, public speaking, and academic writing.

  • English conversation practice
  • Academic writing support
  • Public speaking training

College Preparation

Comprehensive guidance for college applications, financial aid, and academic preparation.

  • SAT/ACT preparation
  • Application essay writing
  • Financial aid guidance

Mentorship Program

One-on-one and group mentorship opportunities connecting students with successful professionals and students.

  • Academic mentoring
  • Career guidance
  • Personal development
