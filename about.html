About Desteeny

Learn about our story, mission, and how we connect people through skill sharing

Our Story

Desteeny was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate individuals who believed that everyone has valuable skills to share and something new to learn. We recognized that in our increasingly connected world, people were still feeling isolated and missing opportunities to connect with others who could teach them new skills.

What started as a small group of friends teaching each other coding, cooking, and languages has grown into a vibrant community where hundreds of people come together to share knowledge, learn from each other, and collaborate on meaningful projects. Our name "Desteeny" represents our commitment to creating a community where everyone can grow together.

Our Values

Sharing

We believe everyone has valuable skills to share and that teaching others is one of the best ways to learn.

Collaboration

We work together with partner organizations and community members to create meaningful impact.

Community

We build strong, inclusive communities where everyone feels welcome to teach and learn.

Growth

We encourage continuous learning and personal growth through skill sharing and collaboration.
